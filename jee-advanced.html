What is JEE Advanced?

It is the second phase of the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE), which is conducted for students who qualify for JEE Main. It is a highly competitive exam that tests students' knowledge, analytical skills, and ability to solve complex problems. Only the top performers in JEE Main are eligible to appear for Advanced, making it one of the toughest exams in India.
