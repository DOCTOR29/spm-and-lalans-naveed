What is the Pre-Foundation Course?

The Pre-Foundation course is a preparatory program for students who are aiming to strengthen their understanding of fundamental subjects such as Math, Science, and logical reasoning.

This course focuses on:

  • Building strong conceptual understanding.
  • Developing problem-solving skills.
  • Preparing students for school-level exams as well as future competitive exams like JEE and NEET.
