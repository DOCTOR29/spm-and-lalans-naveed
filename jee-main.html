What is JEE Main?

JEE Main is the first stage of the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). It serves as a gateway to undergraduate engineering programs in various prestigious colleges. Students who clear JEE-Main can also attempt JEE Advanced, which is necessary for admission to the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). Success in JEE not only opens doors to top colleges but also gives students the confidence to excel in JEE Advanced.
